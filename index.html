LOWER MAINLAND AND VICTORIA DRUGS IN SEWAGE OUTPUT


We flush away many things into our sewers as waste. But did you know that your pharmaceutical waste gets dumped into the environment? And that this is dangerous for your health?

Many Perspectives for a Big Issue

Issues that involve a lot of people can get very complicated . When this happens, the problem requires a wide range of expertise across different fields. Here we have the process of consuming medication (a human factor), how the sewers filter it (a technological factor), and how it gets into the water supply (an environmental factor).
